cHe mAH TUJHE MAAN NE PHOOLON KI TARAH PALA
MAMTA KE UJALON MEIN KHILTA THA TERA CHERA
 SAEHARA KI TRAF Kise kADMO KO BADHAONN MAIN

MAIDANE  MUSIBAT MEIN HAI DAFAN KI TAYARI
EHSAS KI AKHON SE HAI ZAQM JIGAR ZARI
TAQDEER YAHI KAHTI HAI AB  KHAK UDAOON MAIN

MAIN AISA MUSAFIR HOON KHALI HAI MERA DAMAN
PANI KE LIYE BETA MURJHAYA TERA BACHPAN
KUMHLAYA HUA CHERA KIS TRAH BNAON MEIN

HAIDAR KA PISAR HOON MEIN HAI SABAR MERA SHEVA
EHSAS HAI MAGAR MAIN BHI EK BAAP HOON AIE BETA
AB TU HI BATA KAISE IS DIL KO MANON MAIN
   
MAQTAL MEIN UDHAR SOI HUR FAUJ SAQADAT HAI
BEHOSH IDHAR GHAM SE BIMAR MUSIBAT HAI
HAI KAON MERA JISKO IS WAQT BULAOON MAIN
